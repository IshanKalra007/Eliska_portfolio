MY PROJECTS

Mopped Muggers (November 2024)

This video was part of a formative assignment designed to develop my skills in presenting, filming, video editing, and voiceover work. Working alongside a coursemate, I created a comprehensive piece exploring the rise of moped-related crimes. For this project, I was responsible for the on-camera link, filming, editing, and voiceover narration, ensuring a dynamic and engaging final product.
